BUSA

BUSA is your student organisation. Our mission is to make student life at Bond more rewarding, ranging from enhancing academic pursuits, facilitating sporting involvement, and satisfying social necessities. We are a voluntary, apolitical group of elected students here to represent you. We’re here to tie it all together.

We offer a number of services, including:

  • Clubs, Societies and Association support. Bond’s cultural, sporting and special interest clubs offer a wealth of opportunities to meet people who share common interests. There are numerous active clubs on campus, ranging from political parties and religious groups through to drama, debating and sporting societies. BUSA works to provide administrative and financial assistance to maintain the lifeblood of university culture.
  • Events. From ‘North v South’ rugby games, to pub crawls and Thursday Nights at Don’s, BUSA organises a plethora of events throughout the year. These events keep Bondies together and target a number of groups within Bond’s diverse student community.
  • Your voice on University issues. BUSA ensures Bond students have an independent voice in the administration of the University. The Association has a representative on the University’s Academic Senate, which deals with academic policy and discipline on campus, and regularly consults with the Registrar, Deans, Vice-Chancellor and Chancellor to ensure students’ opinions are given due consideration in all matters.
  • Publications. Scope is the weekly newsletter published by BUSA and is the most thorough and up-to-date source of news, reviews and information for students. This newsletter is a Bondie’s best friend and is available at Wednesday by the Water every week.
  • External connections. BUSA works with a number of external organisations to enhance the life of Bond students. This includes accessing valuable discounts, free services and support, and exclusive Bond student privileges.

The Bond University Student Association is here to help you make the most of your time at Bond. We exist to provide services to SAM members and the broader student community.
