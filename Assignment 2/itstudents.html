IT Students

BITS

Bond Information Technology Students (BITS) is a Club that aims to bring Information, Communication and Technology Students together and provide its members with the competitive advantage needed after University. The Club will allow students to gain relevant work experience, network with potential employers, Academia and fellow students and gain recognition for their work outside the traditional classroom. Ultimately, it will provide its members a chance to develop their portfolio by increasing the opportunities within their reach!

BITS will provide its members the opportunity to:

  • Hear first about work experience opportunities offered to Bond ICT Students and potentially gain employment
  • Network with potential employers, Academia and fellow students
  • Gain recognition for their work outside the classroom
  • Attend social events with other likeminded ICT students
  • Receive advice regarding assessment and projects from peers
  • Add to their portfolio to give them the ultimate competitive advantage!
