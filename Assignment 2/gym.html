Gymnasium & Sporting Facilities

Why pay $500 or more for annual gym membership?

Facilities

The Bond University Institute for Sport provides some of the best facilities on campus, all-inclusive when you purchase your SAM. Ranked as the Australian Fitness Club of the Year (2000), you can use all of the sporting facilities on an unlimited basis throughout the semester.

The gymnasium offers both cardio and weights equipment, with personal trainers available for a free consultation. The sports hall can be used for indoor soccer, netball, basketball, badminton and dodge ball, and can be booked in advance.

For sports training, Bondies can also use Bond's other sporting facilities:

  • Beach volleyball courts
  • Golf and cricket practice nets
  • Rowing shed and jetty
  • Rugby & soccer fields
  • Squash courts
  • Tennis courts

Group exercise classes

As a SAM member, you can also join in as many of the group exercise classes as you like. In 091, around 48 classes were held each week! This includes:

  • Boxing circuit
  • Deep water running
  • Pilates
  • Yoga
  • Strength

For class times, see: http://www.bond.edu.au/sport/group-exercise.html

Need a break?

If you're into something a little more relaxing, why not take advantage of the Olympic-size swimming pool and spa which are both open throughout the year. What better way to soak up the Gold Coast sun?

Or, you can get a remedial, Swedish or sports massage by Bond's very own massage therapist. There is also a sauna to help you relax at the end of a busy day.

For more information, please visit: http://www.bond.edu.au/sport/
