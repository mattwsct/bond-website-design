United Nations Student Association

The Bond University United Nations Association (BUUNSA)

The Bond University United Nations Association (BUUNSA) is a student association that offers students with the opportunity to get involved in various aspects of the United Nations.  BUUNSA offers students with the ability to compete in international, as well as domestic model United Nations Conference's.  BUUNSA also runs events that highlight global social justice projects throughout the semester.

BUUNSA's main event is ‘Films that Matter' which is run every Wednesday day from 6pm.  This event provides students with the opportunity to watch a film that highlights various international, social or historical issues followed by a short discussion.

BUUNSA meetings are held every Tuesday at 3pm.  New members are always welcome so please email the contacts below for more information or to be added to the mailing list.
