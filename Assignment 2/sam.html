What is SAM?

The Student Activity Membership (SAM) is available for all Bond students to purchase each semester. It offers a wide range of benefits for all members and is available for students on and off campus.

SAM is a must-have for all Bondies. If you are into sports or social events, after academic representation, or love finding a club you are passionate about, SAM is for you! All SAM funds are directed back into the Bond student community, making YOUR time at Bond more memorable and enjoyable.

What are the benefits?

As a SAM member, you will receive at least 1/3 off the price of all BUSA-sponsored events on campus such as Pub Crawl, Students’ Ball, Palaver and Race Day. It also gives you heavily subsidised tickets to other on-campus events, activities and parties, such as Business v Law Rugby, Palaver and Bondy 500.

In addition, you receive free access to:

  • All of Bond’s sporting facilities
  • Group gym classes
  • Bond's cultural and sporting clubs and societies
  • The student discount card: V-Card
  • BUSA’s weekly ‘Wednesday By The Water’ BBQ
  • Don’s Tavern every week, including special Thursday events

How much does it cost?

Access to all these benefits is available for little more than a dollar a day, or $110 a semester. For the chance to maximise your student experience, it's more than worth it.

How do I purchase SAM?

You can sign up for your SAM anytime during the semester by signing a quick and easy form in the Sports Centre.

If you are a new student, we’ll bring SAM to you! You can purchase your membership at the Information Hub on Monday and Tuesday of Bond Week.

In case you’re not back for Bond Week, you’ll also be able to purchase your SAM in the BUSA or Sports Centre tent at Club Sign on Day on Friday of week 1. Be there to take advantage of all the great offers offered exclusively to Bondies.
