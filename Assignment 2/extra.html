Extra SAM benefits

FREE Don's Tavern

BUSA is dedicated to making your time at Bond more enjoyable, including running sick parties and social functions. Every Thursday night means the end of the week is near and it's time to break out of study mode and party!

Thursdays@Don's is Bondies night. All SAM payers get free entry into Don's, Tavern where you can chillax and play a game of pool or hit it hard for a late night out. We put on live music each week, ranging for live rock bands to Australia's best DJ's. Best of all, we have busses each week out to EAST Nightclub in Broadbeach.

Throughout the semester, keep your eyes open for BUSA's awesome wicked sweet DJ's, including John Course, Timmy Trumpet and the Stafford Brothers, who are on campus exclusively for Bondies.

Make sure you head on down to Don's each semester for End-of-Sem Bash, held on the final day of exams in week 14. As the bell rings marking the end of another semester, make sure you have a pint or a jug in your hand to party the semester away!

Club Activities

The Student Activity Membership also provides you a great range of discounts within Bond's cultural and sporting clubs and societies. This includes free membership to most clubs, heavily subsidised ticket prices and priority access to events.
